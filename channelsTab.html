E's Chinese Class

This channel is dedicated to such fundamental aspects of language learning as grammar, vocabulary and listeting. There are videos about various grammatical topics and rules presented with helpful diagrams and examples. Also the creator makes videos with in depth analysis about Chinese words and characters where their origins and correct usage are explained as well as potential mistakes in writing are mentioned. Videos with stories told slowly can be extremely beneficial especially for beginners because they help to gradually get used to Chinese native speech.

Chou Meizai

It is a channel with short and entartaining videos about Chinese characters. In, on average, 1 minute and 30 seconds you are able to learn about character's history and evolution, what parts it consists of, its stroke order, pronaunciation and meaning. Everything is accompanied by catchy melody and lots of pictures, which are meant to visually resemble characters. Videos are done both in English and Chinese about simplified and traditional characters.

Videos

Grace Mandarin Chinese

This channel is created by a Taiwanese Youtuber and is focused on Mandarin Chinese. There are lots of useful videos about grammar and pronaunciation done in a format that easy to understand and remember. However, not all videos are about traditionally discussed aspects of the language. There are videos with creator explaining different Chinese accents, slang and pop-culture references.

Videos

Jerry Wang

Content of this channel mostly consists of Youtube Shorts but there are many longer videos as well. The creator emphasizes vocabulary and topics related to business Chinese. On the other hand, there are still many videos about different sides of Chinese culture. For example, cuisine, idioms, set expressions, TV series and music.
